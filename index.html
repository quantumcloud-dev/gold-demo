Message of the Punong Barangay

My warmest greeting to all my kabarangay!
serving the people of Pasay, extending assistance to fellow Punong Barangays and supporting the vision of the officials of the city is both an honor and privilege. As Punong Barangay of Barngay 173, President of the Liga ng mga Barangay sa Pilipinas, Pasay City Chapter and representative (Councilor) of all 201 Punong Barangays in the Sanggunian Panglungsod ng Pasay is a gargantuan task, yet performing the duties of a public servant is a satisfying and fulfilling job.

A barangay is a community of people with different cultures, needs, problems and others. Hence, it plays a crucial role in the community in the maintenance of peace and orde, securing the general welfare of the people and barangay development. Assisting, helping, acting and responding to the needs of the people are expected of you as a public official. Service to the barangay is from womb to tomb, and from disaster to maana. But, the most meaningful governance is interacting with people from all walks of life (in thhe barangay as well as the in the city) and be able to be an instrument of change in their lives. Service to the public is service to the country and to God Almighty.

About the Liga

The Liga ng mga Barangay sa Pilipinas, Pasay City Chapter is an aggroupment of all the 201 barangays of the city of Pasay which are in turn represented by their respective Punong Barangays or Barangay Chairmen. The Local Government Code of the Philippines enunciates the legal basis of the existence of the existence of the Liga ng mga Barangay. The primary purpose of the Liga ng mga Barangay, is the determination of the representation of the Liga ng mga Barangay in the Sangunian Panglungsod of Pasay and the ventilation, articulation and crystallization of issues affecting barangay government administration and securing solutions thereto, through proper and legal means.

In addition, the Liga ng mga Barangay has one principal aim, namely: to promote the development of barangays and secure the general welfare of the inhabitants. The Liga ng mga Barangay is the vehicle assigned to make new development approach materialize and produce results.

— Hon. Julie Gonzales

About Us

Former Pasay City Mayor Pablo Cuneta first created 487 barangays in the city. Presidential Republic Act No. 3590, Presidential Decrees Nos. 86 and 210 paved the way of the appointment of an officer-in-charge(OIC) for each barangay. Mayor Cuneta later on reduced the number to 200 barangays. Additional one barangay that is Barangay 201 at present was created by an ordinance.

Mayor Cuneta appointed Zosimo Papa as OIC Barangay Chairman of Barangay 173. When the later died, he was replaced by Rodolfo Papa and assumed the position of Barangay Chairman of Barangay 173. Oscar Raymundo, who won in the election but failed to finish his term. Eduardo Verdadero ran in the subsequent election and lost to Roger Mendigo. Following the next election, Rolando Papa won the seat but later died during his term. He was succeeded by his first Kagawad Julie G. Gonzales.

Julie G. Gonzales won in the succeeding elections. sheis on her last term of office. Punong Barangay Julie Gonzales is also elected president of the Liga ng mga Barangay sa Pilipinas, Pasay City Chapter, representing the 201 barangays of the city to the National Liga. She is currently the Auditor of the National Liga(NCR). Being the Liga president, she automatically becomes ex-officio member of the Sanggunian Panglungsod(City council) ng Pasay, hence people call her Councilor Julie.
