About Us

In Pasay City former Mayor Pablo Cuneta first created 487 barangays. By virtue of PD 86 and PD 210 appointed an officer in charge (OIC) for each barangay. Mayor Pablo Cuneta, later reduced the number to 200 and further to 201 barangays.

In Barangay 173, Mayor Pablo Cuneta appointed Mr. Zosimo Papa as OIC otherwise known as Barangay Chairman. When Zosimo Papa died, Rodolfo Papa is the one who take place as a Baragay Chairman. After that, the election comes and Oscar Raymundo run as a Barangay Chairman. He win with highest votes and he become a barangay chairman. He did not finish his term as a barangay chairman so that Eduardo Verdadero will take place. Then the election come again Eduardo Verdado run as a barangay chairman but he failed to win, so that Roger Mendigo was the one who win as a Barangay Chairman.

Election in the barangay level again ensued and Rolando Papa win as Barangay Chairman. When Rolando Papa died the first kagawad Julie G. Gonzales succeeded it. Then the next election was held Julie G. Gonzales retain as Barangay Chairman up to present.
